There is a rising emphasis on content-driven films, and the business has become more diversified with the representation of varied tales and personalities on screen. The article will discuss the top 10 highest-grossing movies in India, which had a significant impact on the worldwide box office collections.

Top 10 Highest-Grossing Movies in India

1. Dangal

Dangal is one of the highest-grossing Indian movies of all time. It was directed by Nitesh Tewari, with Amir Khan as the major lead. It tells the true story of an ex-wrestler, Mahavir Singh Phogat, and his struggle to achieve his dreams due to familial and societal pressure to get employed in a more traditional job. However, the fighter vows to teach his son how to wrestle and fulfill his dreams through him. However, when he finds himself only having daughters, his dreams are challenged only to be redirected in a new direction. This is an inspiring movie about family, achieving hard things, and the journey of it all. Full of details, great direction, acting, and soundtracks, Dangal has been a massive success, amassing around $300 million at the international box office.

The movie has become the most successful Bollywood production ever. And right now, it is a serious contender to make the top 1000 Indian movies in box office revenue.

IMDB Rating: 9/10

2. Bahubali 2

This film is a sequel action-fantasy and drama piece that is produced so highly that it became Indias biggest blockbuster movie. It was directed by S. S. Rajamouli and starring Prabhas and Rana Daggubati. It tells the story of the son of Bahubali, Shiva, who starts to look for answers after he learns about his heritage and the ways his parents and his people were wronged and betrayed, which leads to a battle against a brutal tyrant. Baahubali 2: The Conclusion was one of the most expensive Indian movies to be ever made with an investment of around $37 million. Yet, its mega-success has made this financial investment all the more worth it, as the movie has gathered around $270 million dollars. It has been praised for its visual appealfrom great costume and set designs to amazing special effects. .

IMDB Rating: 9.5/10

3. Pathaan

Pathaan, starring SRK, earned 100 crores on its first day, making it the highest Hindi film opening of all time. Not only that, but within the first week of its theatrical run, the King Khan-starring film “Pathaan” entered the Rs 200- and 300-crore clubs.

The movie has become the most successful Bollywood production ever. And right now, it is a serious contender to make the top 1000 Indian movies in box office revenue.

IMDB Rating: 6.6/10

4. RRR 

RRR is an Oscar-nominated movie directed by S. S. Rajamouli with its main stars being Rama Rao and Ram Charan. Set in India in 1920, it tells the fictional story of two men who choose to fight for their countries due to different motives. However, when the two accidentally meet, they bond, but their differing true identities would only stir drama if the cover gets blown. This action-packed drama combines dancing, romance, and comedy which makes for a compelling movie. The film has amassed $160 million worldwide..

IMDB Rating: 7.9/10 

5. KGF: Chapter 2

K.G.F. Chapter 2 is a sequel to the movie K.G.G. Part 1. It was directed by Prashanth Neel and stars Naveen Kumar, Sanjay Dutt and Raveena Tandon. The film follows the story of Rocky, a leader admired by many who manages to become the ruler of Kolar Gold Fields. However, his popularity and influence are objected to by the government as they see him as a threat to law and order. This, in turn, throws him into blood-soaked, challenging battles. K.G.G. is yet another action-packed movie that has gained an even higher rating than RRR overall, amassing around $90 million worldwide..

IMDB Rating: 9.5/10

6. Brahmastra Part One: Shiva

The highly anticipated Alia Bhatt-Ranbir Kapoor film was a box office hit. The film enchanted spectators with its audio-visual effects and plot, which was based on mythology. The movie “Brahmastra,” starring Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor, is not based on a real-life event; rather, it is fiction that makes use of the “astraverse” concept. The movie has a projected global box office gross of over 431 crores (US$54 million), making it the highest-earning Hindi film of 2022, the fifth-highest-grossing Indian movie of 2022, and the 20th-highest-grossing Indian movie overall. 

IMDB Rating: 5.6/10

7. Kantara

Everyone was surprised by the movie Kantara. Without any overt attempt to please a PAN-Indian audience, the film became a smash hit due to its original plot and breathtaking images. The low-budget movie Kantara, whose name means “mystical woodland,” was intended to depict the enthralling “Bhoota Kola” legend from the hinterlands of Karnataka. In the end, however, Rishab Shetty’s remarkable performance and its genuine portrayal of culture won plaudits. It was launched with a Hindi dubbing for viewers in the north, and it has not looked back since. Despite being dogged by controversy, the movie, which had a meagre 16 crore rupee budget, has so far made 400 crore rupees.

IMDB Rating: 8.3/10

8. Ponniyin Selvan-I

Legendary filmmaker Mani Ratnam assembled a spectacular star cast for this masterpiece film, featuring Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Trisha Krishnan, Vikram, and others. Maniratnam made an effort to brilliantly capture the depth of Chola’s history. Vandiyathevan, a charming, brave, and gallant young man, is the protagonist of the tale. He travels across the Chola territory to deliver a letter from Crown Prince Aditya Karikalan to Emperor Sundara Chola and Princess Kundavai. The movie made Rs 500 crore globally.

IMDB Rating: 8.3/10

9. The Kashmir Files

The Kashmir Files make an effort to depict the tragic tale of the Kashmiri Pandit Community’s catastrophic flight. The Kashmir Files is innovative and does not treat its audience like a given. The popularity of the film demonstrates that the audience is seeking entertainment beyond the mind-numbing Bollywood offerings, thanks to its intricate dialogue and multifaceted characters. This film grossed Rs 344.2 crore at the global box office.

IMDB Rating: 9.5/10

10. Vikram

One would have to look very hard to find an over-the-top action film that is better than “Vikram,” one of the best films of the year. High-action drama Vikram, starring Kamal Haasan, Fahadh Fasil, Vijay Sethupathi, and Narain, has a captivating background score. In a high-octane action film, a special investigator is tasked with investigating a case of serial homicides. As he continues to look into the situation, he realises that it is not as straightforward as it first seems and that continuing in this direction can only lead to violence between all parties. Globally, the movie brought in $424 million.

IMDB Rating: 8.3/10

11. Drishyam 2

In 2022, Vijay Salgaonkar and his family return to the big screen with this part. A popular movie’s second instalment did well at the box office. The same director as Drishyam 1, Jeethu Joseph, has excellent direction and a well-written screenplay. Each cast member’s acting in the movie is engaging. The success of the movie was mostly due to the originality of Ajay Devgn’s Drishyam 2 and the fact that it was not a remake of Mohanlal’s Malayalam original. Audiences returned to the theatres in large numbers thanks to Drishyam 2. It brought in 311 crore rupees globally.

IMDB Rating: 7.7/10

12. Gangubai Kathiawadi

Bollywood director Sanjay Leela Bhansali is known for his extraordinary creativity and production values. The narrative of a little girl who became a courageous icon for prostitutes after being confined in the biggest brothel in Mumbai was told with nuance and Bhansali’s attractive artistic sense. In this film, Alia Bhatt, who played the lead role, displayed her acting talent. Globally, it generated around Rs 200 crore.

IMDB Rating: 7.8/10
